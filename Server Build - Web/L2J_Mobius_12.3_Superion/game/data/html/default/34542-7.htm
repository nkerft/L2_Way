Manager of Party Instance Zones Benusta:
Your goal is to defeat Ramona in the Empress' Palace.
To enter the Empress' Palace, you need a party of 2-7 Lv. 115+ characters. Before you enter, you need to pick a difficulty setting. You can stay in the Empress' Palace for 6 minutes.
If you defeat Ramona, you won't be able to enter the Empress' Palace until it resets at 6:30 the next Wednesday.
Also, keep in mind that you can reenter the zone during battle, but if all the party members have left the zone, you need to return within 1 minute.
